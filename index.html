BIO

Joe Burrow is the starting Quarterback for the Cincinnati Bengals. He was selected with the first overall pick in the 2020 nfl draft. He was a college standout at LSU, where he won the Heisman Trophy as well as a National Championship. He has played four seasons with the bengals. He has a 29-22-1 record with the Bengals. With his incredible talent, and calm demeanor he is set to bring the bengals their first ever lombardie. He is currently on his second contract earning an average of 55,000,000 dollars a year.
